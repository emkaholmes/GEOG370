By: Emma Holmes

My Map


I created this map to answer the question "How many health sites are in each city with a population over 100,000 in Bosnia and Herzegovina?"
To answer this, I created a 15 kilometer buffer around each of the city points then used intersection to get the healthsites that were only inside these buffers. I used intersection instead of clip so that the data of which city the health site is in would not be lost. I then used queries to select and export the health sites within each city to their own data.
